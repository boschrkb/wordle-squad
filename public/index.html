HOW TO PLAY
The Official Daily Setup Guide
1
Finish your Wordle ⬛🟨🟩
Complete today's puzzle in the Wordle app as normal.
2
Tap Share → tap COPY ⚠️
This is critical — you must tap Copy (not just Share) so your score is on the clipboard. Do this before opening WhatsApp.
3
Open WhatsApp → paste to the group 💬
Long-press the message box and paste your score into the group chat.
4
Tap the pinned link to open Wordle Squad 📌
Find the pinned message in the WhatsApp group and tap the link to open this app.
5
Tap "📋 Read Clipboard" — score appears automatically ✨
On the Today's Round tab, tap the paste button. Your round and result are detected instantly.
6
Enter your name → tap Submit 🏌️
Select your name from the dropdown and tap Post Score. You're on the leaderboard!
⚠️ Forgot to Copy first? No problem — go back to Today's Round and paste your Wordle share text manually in the box at the bottom of the page.

THE WORDLE OPEN
Official Rules & Regulations
Est. 2021 · All rights reserved · Appeals not accepted
RULE 1
The Season 🗓️

The Wordle Open is contested over exactly 18 rounds. Each day's Wordle is one round. The current season runs from Round 1754 to Round 1771. When Round 1772 arrives, a new season begins automatically.

There is no off-season. There is no mercy.

RULE 2
Scoring 🏌️

Your guess count is your stroke count. Low score wins. This is golf.

⛳ 1/6Hole in One−3
🦅 2/6Eagle−2
🐦 3/6Birdie−1
⚪ 4/6ParE
😬 5/6Bogey+1
😰 6/6Double Bogey+2
💀 X/6Penalty Stroke+3

* Failed puzzles count as 7 strokes. The course does not care about your feelings.

RULE 3
Par 🟢

Par is 4 strokes per round, making the full 18-hole season par 72.

Finish under 72 and you are among the greats. Finish over 72 and we don't talk about it at dinner.

RULE 4
Weekly Champion 🏆

The player with the lowest total strokes each week earns a place in the Champions Locker Room — the most prestigious hall in recreational Wordle history.

Weekly glory is fleeting. Season glory is eternal.

RULE 5
Sudden Death Playoff ⚡

If two or more players finish the 18-round season with an identical total score, a sudden death playoff commences immediately within normal daily play. No additional rounds are scheduled. Life continues. So does the tournament.

Playoff rounds are the same daily Wordle, played under the full weight of competitive pressure.

RULE 6
Playoff Elimination 🔴

In a 3-way (or more) playoff, any player who scores higher than the lowest score that day is immediately eliminated.

EXAMPLE
Day 1 — Alice: 2 · Bob: 2 · Charlie: 4
→ Charlie eliminated (scored above the low of 2)
→ Alice and Bob continue to Day 2

Day 2 — Alice: 3 · Bob: 5
→ Bob eliminated · Alice wins the season 🏆

⚠️ Once eliminated you cannot re-enter the playoff. The Committee is not accepting appeals, emails, or phone calls at this time.

RULE 7
The Green Jacket 🏌️

The season champion receives eternal glory, their name immortalised in the Champions Locker Room, and the unspoken respect of their peers for a period not to exceed one calendar week.

The Committee congratulates all participants on their participation.

⛳ Good luck. Keep your head down.
🌿 Mind the rough. Play the ball as it lies.
